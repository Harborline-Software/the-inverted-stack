# Stage 4 - Scaffolding

Skeleton draft. Section headers, beats expanded to topic sentences, code-snippet stubs marked, citation placeholders inserted. Prose quality is not the goal here - completeness is.

**Input:** Approved package design.

**Artifacts:**
- `NN-short-slug/scaffold.md` - the skeleton itself.
- `NN-short-slug/open-questions.md` *(optional)* - anything that surfaced during scaffolding and needs author input.

**Tools:** `chapter-drafter`, `technical-book-writer`.

**Exit criterion:** Every section in the design has a topic sentence; every code snippet has a stub or `// illustrative - not runnable` marker; every claim has a `<!-- CITE: ? -->` marker or a real citation.
