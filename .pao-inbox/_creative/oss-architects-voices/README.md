# OSS Architects' Voices - Voice Reference Library

This directory holds **complete in-universe documents** from the OSS local-first architects' side (Sunfish project; Joel, Anna, Wanjiru, Priya; the broader OSS distributed-systems community). It is the **protagonist-side balance** to `forsaken-position-papers/`.

Both libraries serve the chapter epigraph device. The series alternates between voices: a Forsaken position lands the centralizing argument before an OSS-side document lands the counter; or vice versa. The reader hears both sides at full conviction; the architectural correctness emerges through the cumulative weight of evidence rather than through narrator advocacy.

## Series-design constraint - these documents must be plausible

The OSS side, like the Forsaken side, must be sincere, operationally informed, and architecturally serious. The series' challenge is not making the OSS architects sympathetic; it is making them **credibly correct without making them rhetorically heavy-handed.** A protagonist-side document that reads as preaching makes the architectural triumph feel asserted rather than earned. A document that engages the Forsaken's argument on its own terms - and answers it - earns the position.

The constraint:
- **Sincere** - the architects genuinely believe the position
- **Operationally informed** - the position engages with real deployment problems, including the ones the Forsaken side raises
- **Architecturally serious** - the engineering is competent
- **Specifically responsive** - the position answers actual Forsaken arguments rather than generic centralization-fear talking points

## Documents in this directory

1. `01-annex-a-minority-opinion.md` - Annex A: Minority Opinion appended to the Working Group regulatory filing (`forsaken-position-papers/03-regulatory-filing.md`). Multi-signatory; OSS architects rebutting the Working Group's recommendation. ~720 words; institutional-formal voice; February 2027.
