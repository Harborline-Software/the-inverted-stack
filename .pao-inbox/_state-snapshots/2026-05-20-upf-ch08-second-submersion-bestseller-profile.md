**Grade: C-minus.** First-person past tense is maintained correctly throughout. Anna

### LD-2: Tone-Diction

**Grade: D.** The current draft's dominant mode is bureaucratic enumeration. Sample:
"She had the manila folder open in her left hand and a tablet in her right and the satchel
that lived on the bench at the wardroom desk over her shoulder." This is grammatically

to say") does not appear once in the chapter. The chapter sounds like an official ops

### LD-3: Multi-Sense Imagery

**Grade: C.** The draft contains isolated sensory moments — "the wardroom hum was the
wardroom hum" (auditory, though repeated to the point of tic), the clock reading, the

temperature senses are absent. Antarctica under the ice at 300m is a sensory environment
