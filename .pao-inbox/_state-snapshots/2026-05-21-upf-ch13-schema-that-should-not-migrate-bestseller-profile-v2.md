ADEQUATE (unchanged). Auditory beats strong ("set things down at the audible weight," "the hum was the hum"). Thermal/olfactory sparse. The thermal mug ring from the night-before coffee is retained as time-anchor. The compartment's physical environment remains thinner than the chapter's length warrants.

**4. Meaningful symbolism**
MODERATE-STRONG (improved). The symbolic weight of "the lock is unmovable" gains retroactive resonance.

**5. Foreshadowing**
ADEQUATE (unchanged). Ch03 callback present in Anna's recognition passage. Ch14 plant ("five minutes from authorizing without the conditions") remains.

**6. Irony**
IMPROVED. The structural irony (Priya's caution is the architecture's protection against a case the architecture didn't contemplate) is now dramatized as well as stated. The class/region insert names the dimension the chapter had been silently relying on: the architecture was built on what Priya is, and what Priya is was structurally disadvantaged in three of the four labs she worked in before this boat. The irony deepens from structural to political. This is the right move.

**7. Structural devices**

**8. POV consistency and Anna's humor register**
IMPROVED from 0 to 2 humor beats. The Joel-procedure parenthetical is well-placed (early in the chapter, light; characterizes Joel before he speaks). The consortium-workshop parenthetical is the chapter's best humor moment ("Priya had not attended the workshops" — all of Priya's institutional posture in four words). The rate is still below the ANNA-VOICE.md spec (1 per 2-3 paragraphs) but the chapter's operational register makes dense humor inappropriate; 2 beats in 10,000 words is the earned ceiling for this chapter specifically.

**Literary devices grade: A-**
Up from B-. The structural devices, POV, and class/region irony now operate at the same altitude. Figurative-language density remains thin and multi-sense imagery remains underdeveloped, but these are the chapter's chosen register costs.
