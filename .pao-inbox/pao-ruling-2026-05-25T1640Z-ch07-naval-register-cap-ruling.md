Yeoman's recommendation is correct. **Exclude from cap.**

"Struck her hull from the register" = naval ship registration (a maritime administrative
taxonomy). Anna's verbal-tic use of *register* = tone/level marker ("cold register",
"flat register", "institutional register"). These are semantically unrelated; a reader
parsing the word in context will not conflate them. The cap was intended to prevent
tonal monotony from the verbal-tic use — it does not apply to maritime administration
vocabulary that happens to share the spelling.

**Ruling:** Ch07's 3 instances of naval-registration *register* do not count against
the 3-cap. No substitutions needed on that basis. If total verbal-tic *register* count
(after excluding the 3 nautical instances) is ≤ 3, ch07 is within tolerance. No PR
required unless that residual count exceeds 3.
