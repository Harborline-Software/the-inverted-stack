1. **The lexical fatigue families YAML** at `vol-2/_glossary/_lexical_fatigue_families.yaml` already flags `discipline` with the note: *"Thematic word; check density vs narrative-essential threshold."* For Ch 07 specifically, the narrative-essential threshold is high — the word is the chapter's organizing concept. Trimming `discipline` from this beat weakens the recognition; the recognition is the chapter's payoff.

The repeated `discipline` is character voice, not narrator habit — Joel's nuclear-Navy register reads as authentic precisely because the word recurs.
